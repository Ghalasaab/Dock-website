Logistics, Simplified in 3 Smart Steps

1. Shipment Initiated

As soon as the merchant places an order, DOCK captures all relevant details instantly. The system prepares the shipment for automated routing and compliance checks. No manual entry is required—everything is ready for the next step.

2. AI-Powered Routing

DOCK’s AI analyzes all available carriers and routes in real-time. It selects the most efficient, cost-effective, and reliable option. This ensures that every delivery is optimized for speed, cost, and reliability.

3. Automated Delivery Assignment

Once the optimal route and carrier are chosen, the delivery is assigned immediately. Merchants can track the shipment in real-time, while DOCK handles everything behind the scenes. This cuts logistics time significantly and reduces manual workload.
